Owning the Daily Commute How I Used Lift Media to Build Hyperlocal Brand Visibility Across Premium Societies in Gurgaon

BrandNaturally Good Organic Farms
RoleBrand Marketing & Growth Manager
CampaignHyperlocal Lift Media Campaign
ObjectiveIncrease brand awareness, improve local visibility, and generate high-intent leads by placing Naturally Good directly in front of its target audience.

The Challenge

Naturally Good caters to a very specific audience—health-conscious families living in premium residential societies. Traditional digital advertising helped us reach people online, but it didn't always create a strong local presence or trust within the communities we wanted to serve. The challenge was simple: How do we make Naturally Good a familiar name before customers even think about buying organic vegetables? We needed a marketing channel that reached residents repeatedly, in a space where they weren't distracted by endless scrolling or competing advertisements.

The Insight

One thing stood out during our customer research—our ideal customers spend time in apartment lifts every single day. Whether they were leaving for work, returning home, or heading out for errands, the lift offered a few uninterrupted seconds of attention. Instead of competing for attention online, we decided to own this everyday moment.

The Strategy

I planned and executed a large-scale lift media campaign across premium residential societies in Gurgaon. The objective wasn't just visibility—it was consistent brand recall. By placing Naturally Good inside residential lifts, we ensured that residents saw the brand multiple times every day, gradually building familiarity and trust. The campaign focused on premium communities that closely matched our target audience, allowing us to reach families with a higher likelihood of subscribing to our farm-to-home service.

My Role

I managed the campaign from planning to on-ground execution. My responsibilities included:

  • Identifying premium residential societies aligned with our target audience
  • Coordinating with lift media vendors and society management teams
  • Planning campaign timelines and installation schedules
  • Designing and reviewing creative communication for the posters
  • Ensuring consistent branding across all locations
  • Monitoring installation quality and placement
  • Conducting regular follow-ups and audits to ensure posters remained visible throughout the

campaign One of the biggest milestones of this project was successfully overseeing the installation of more than 500 lift posters across premium residential societies in Gurgaon. Managing a campaign of this scale required careful coordination between multiple stakeholders while maintaining consistency in execution.

Creative Approach

Since residents only spend a few seconds inside a lift, every poster had to communicate its message instantly. The creatives focused on:

  • Fresh, farm-to-home organic vegetables
  • Simple and benefit-led messaging
  • Strong visual hierarchy
  • QR codes for quick action
  • Clear calls-to-action encouraging app downloads and inquiries

Rather than overwhelming viewers with information, the communication was designed to spark curiosity and make Naturally Good memorable.

Marketing Principles Applied

Hyperlocal Marketing

Instead of targeting a broad audience, the campaign focused on neighborhoods where our ideal customers lived. This improved relevance and reduced wasted impressions.

High-Frequency Brand Exposure

Marketing research shows that repeated exposure builds familiarity and trust. Residents encountered Naturally Good several times a day, reinforcing brand recall without feeling intrusive.

Contextual Advertising

The campaign placed the brand in an environment where consumers had a few uninterrupted moments to absorb the message, increasing the likelihood of recall.

Offline Meets Online

Each poster acted as a bridge between offline awareness and digital engagement through QR codes and direct response mechanisms, making it easy for interested residents to learn more or take action.

Business Impact

The lift media campaign significantly strengthened Naturally Good's visibility within its core markets. Key outcomes included:

  • Established a strong hyperlocal presence across premium residential communities
  • Increased brand recall through repeated daily exposure
  • Improved awareness among high-intent households
  • Supported lead generation efforts by driving residents to digital channels
  • Strengthened trust by creating a visible presence within the communities we served
  • Complemented digital marketing efforts with a scalable offline awareness strategy

Beyond impressions, the campaign helped Naturally Good become a recognizable brand in the very neighborhoods where our customers lived.
